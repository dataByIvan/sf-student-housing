# sf-student-housing
Interactive map helping SF college students find housing near USFCA, SFSU, and UCSF (Parnassus, Mission Bay)!\



Points of interest:
USFCA (Lone Mountain / Hilltop campus)
UCSF (Parnassus, Mission Bay — it has multiple, which matters)
SFSU (19th Ave campus)


The inspiration for this project was ignited by my friend's, peer's, and classmate's situation at USFCA, where the juniors and seniors are being kicked out of the dorms to make room for the incoming freshman's. This makes sense because for those who already spent 2-3 years at USFCA, there's a sunk cost fallacy where they will commit to getting that degree here.

Now, I am a data science major and student here at USFCA. But this project include both data science and computer science elements to it.

Essentially, I want to focus on major colleges in SF, like USFCA, UCSF, and SFSU. Not so much for community college, as I doubt there are many people who are moving to SF for the community colleges.

I want it to have elements such as metrics like: rent prices in specific neighborhoods, distance to school via google maps. Euclidean distance would not make sense, as travelling anywhere in the modern world is not as simple as walking in a straight line, rather people have to commute via public transport or car or bike etc. So I want the neighborhood of choice to have some type of scoring of like rent prices, commute times, and connected to actual houses/apartments listed (June 2026).
